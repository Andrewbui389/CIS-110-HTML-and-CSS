About us

We are a local business in Portland, Oregon; this upcoming year marks marks our 100th anniversary. I opened this place right after I graduated University of Oregon.
My parent's are originally from Huế Vietnam and I always loved my mom's home cooking. So I thought why not bring a taste of home to Oregon.
