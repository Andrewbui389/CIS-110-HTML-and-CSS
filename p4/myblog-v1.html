Portland

Beautiful city, unique people and delicious food. I've grown up in Portland, Oregon ever since I was born and stayed here until I left for college. Lots of fun little things to do and explore, hiking is only 20 minutes away, cliff jumping is only 30 minutes away, a short trip to have some fun in nature is always a little ways away. The city itself hold lots of hidden gems, you'll have to keep exploring to find all of them!

Yellow Stone National park

Nature, beautiful forestry, and just miles and miles of wilderness. Different animals that you get to see up close such as buffalos, birds, and much more. While I was there we had a scary moment where the van we were traveling in kept overheating and we were 30 miles out from civilization. We were able to get out of Yellowstone and go to a mechanic. Only to be stranded once more in salt lake city. But thats a story for another time.

Orange County

Sunny and gorgeous roughly 40 minutes south of Los Angelas, I love travel and Orange County is one of my most favorite place to travel to. The city is a nice little getaway to reimmerse myself into Vietnamese culture. It has plenty of Vietnamese restaurants and just a large population of Vietnamese people.
